Create a game show-like front end application that has an interphase that draws inspiration from the game show set pictured. It also draws inspiration from the online "Who Wants to Be a Millionaire" game in the layout of the visual elements. It enables a question and answer format and enables a user to click one of two buttons "Move" or "Stay". The background which as a bluish theme (like the attached image) fills the entire screen and the visual elements (typography, icons, questions, buttons etc) are laid out within the middle 80% width of the screen with 10% padding on either side. The buttons "Move" or "Stay" are at the bottom of the screen. The "stay" button is gray, while the "move" button is pink. When the "move" button is pressed, the answer lights up as the correct answer and takes on a similar pink background, changing from it's traditional white text on blue background.
The questions are supplied from a question bank and the responder's goal is to achieve a 70% score. Each question carries 5 points. The questioner clicks "Stay" or "Move" depending on whether they like the answer the responder gave. Every time the questioner clicks "move", the responder gets 5 points. They win the game when they're at 70 points.
